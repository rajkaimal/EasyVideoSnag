# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Short Description (132 chars max)

Detect and download videos from any webpage. Works on Reddit, Twitter/X, Vimeo, and sites with HTML5 video. No account needed.

## Full Description

EasyVideoSnag automatically detects videos on any webpage and lets you download them with one click.

HOW IT WORKS
When you visit a page with video, EasyVideoSnag scans the page and monitors network activity to find all video sources. A red badge on the extension icon shows how many videos were found. Click the icon to see the list and download any video.

FEATURES
- Detects HTML5 video elements, embedded players, and direct video links
- Catches streaming video loaded by JavaScript (Reddit, Twitter/X, and more)
- Works on Reddit with full audio support (when available)
- One-click download with correct file naming and extension
- Real-time detection as pages load new content
- Clean, simple popup showing all detected videos
- No account, no sign-up, no tracking

SUPPORTED SITES
Works on any website with standard video. Special support for:
- Reddit (v.redd.it videos with audio)
- Twitter/X
- Vimeo, Dailymotion, Twitch
- Streamable, Facebook, TikTok (embedded)
- Any site with HTML5 video or direct video file links

SUPPORTED FORMATS
MP4, WebM, OGG, MOV, AVI, MKV, HLS, DASH

OPEN SOURCE
EasyVideoSnag is fully open source. You can review every line of code at:
https://github.com/rajkaimal/EasyVideoSnag

PRIVACY
EasyVideoSnag does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All detection happens locally in your browser. No analytics, no tracking, no external servers. Your browsing history and downloaded files stay on your device.

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
- "Read and change all your data on all websites" — needed to scan page content for video elements and intercept video network requests. No data is collected or sent anywhere.
- "Manage your downloads" — needed to save videos to your computer when you click Download.
