# EasyVideoSnag Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Data Collection

EasyVideoSnag does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

## How It Works

EasyVideoSnag runs entirely in your browser. It scans webpage content and monitors network requests to detect video sources. All processing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

## What the Extension Accesses

- **Webpage content**: The extension reads the DOM of web pages you visit to find video elements. This data is processed locally and never leaves your browser.
- **Network requests**: The extension observes network requests to detect video file URLs. These URLs are stored temporarily in browser session storage (cleared when you close the tab) and are never transmitted externally.
- **Downloads**: When you click Download, the extension uses Chrome's built-in download manager to save the video file to your computer.

## What the Extension Does NOT Do

- Does not collect browsing history
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not communicate with any external server
- Does not store any data beyond the current browser session
- Does not use cookies

## Data Storage

Video detection results are stored in Chrome's session storage, keyed by tab ID. This data is automatically cleared when you close the tab, navigate away, or restart the browser. No data persists between browser sessions.

## Third Parties

EasyVideoSnag does not share data with any third parties because it does not collect any data.
